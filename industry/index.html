How it works

Four steps to a floor-ready team.

1

Step 01

Design training for your brand

Build scenarios around your menus, service standards, and the real guest interactions your team will face. AI-assisted authoring means you can create realistic, on-brand training — and update it instantly across every location.

2

Step 02

Staff practice anytime, anywhere

On any device, between shifts, in the breakroom, before the lunch rush. From menu knowledge and upselling to service skills and guest interactions — every format is built to drive repetition, so your team builds confidence fast without pulling people off the floor.

3

Step 03

Skills become instinctive

Game mechanics bring staff back voluntarily — and repetition builds the muscle memory a one-off training never can. They learn from mistakes, get instant feedback, and arrive at every shift confident, competent, and floor-ready.

4

Step 04

Connect training to business performance

Data that reveals where skills are strong and where standards are slipping — across every location and role. Connect training directly to the operational metrics that matter: CSAT, upsell rates, audit scores, retention.

Platform features

Purpose-built for performance.

The tools hospitality teams need to onboard faster, deliver consistent service excellence, and scale confidently.

Engage

Game-based simulations

Realistic, on-brand simulations built around your service standards, menus, and guest scenarios, driving the kind of repetition that turns practice into mastery.

Practice

AI role-play

Dynamic AI-powered conversation practice built around realistic hospitality scenarios. Staff rehearse complaints, service recovery, and upselling with characters that respond and adapt naturally.

Access

Mobile-first delivery

Available on iOS and Android. Staff train on their own devices, between shifts, without a desktop or classroom. No scheduling, no friction, no pulling people off the floor.

Scale

50+ language support

Training delivered in over 50 languages. Every team member learns in their native language — ensuring consistent standards across multilingual, multi-site operations.

Measure

Real-time performance data

Reveal competence gaps and performance trends by location, region, or role — in real time. Spot where standards are slipping before it shows up in guest feedback.

Integrate

Connects with your existing tech stack.

Connects with Workday, Docebo, Cornerstone, and other leading LMS and HRIS platforms via SSO and SCIM. No rip-and-replace required.

Certify

Audit-ready completion trails.

Automated records and exports for internal audits, brand standards visits, and regulatory checks — who trained, when, and to what score — without spreadsheets or manual follow-up.

Evolve

Update content in hours, not quarters.

When menus, promotions, or policies change, push refreshed scenarios to every site in one action — so teams always practise what is live on the floor today, not last season’s PDF.
